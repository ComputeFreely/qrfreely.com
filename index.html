Free QR codes without redirects.

QR Freely is a free QR code generator for links, Wi-Fi networks, vCard contacts, email, phone numbers, SMS messages, calendar events, geographic locations, and plain text. It creates PNG and SVG QR codes locally in your browser, so there is no account, no upload step, and no tracking redirect.

Direct payloads

Encode the destination itself, not a hosted redirect URL. Scanners go to the website, Wi-Fi login, contact card, event, location, or message you created.

Local generation

The app builds the QR code in the browser. Your QR content and optional logo image do not need to leave your device.

Export choices

Download PNG for quick use or SVG for scalable print and design workflows. Adjust colors, dot style, finder markers, quiet zone, border, and label.

Does QR Freely upload QR code content?

No. QR Freely generates the QR code in the browser and does not upload the payload.

Can I make Wi-Fi and vCard QR codes?

Yes. QR Freely includes presets for Wi-Fi networks, vCard contacts, URLs, email, phone, SMS, events, locations, and plain text.

Does QR Freely add a tracking redirect?

No. The encoded payload goes directly into the downloaded QR code.
